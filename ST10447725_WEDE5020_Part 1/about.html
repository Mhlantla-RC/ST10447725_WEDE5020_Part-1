About The Odd Jobbers

Our Journey & What Drives Us

The Odd Jobbers was founded in 2025 out of a simple observation: many people need help with small, often overlooked tasks like moving a couch, delivering a parcel, or picking up a bulky purchase. We started as a community-driven initiative, connecting neighbours with reliable individuals, and quickly grew into a platform dedicated to efficiency and trust.

Our mission is to simplify everyday logistics by providing a seamless and reliable platform that connects individuals and businesses with skilled local "jobbers" for their moving and delivery needs. We envision a community where help is always just a click away, fostering local economic activity and making life easier for everyone.

Our Core Values

Reliability

We connect you with jobbers who are punctual and committed to completing tasks efficiently.

Efficiency

Our platform streamlines the process of finding help, booking, and payment.

Community Focus

We empower local individuals to earn income and assist their neighbours.

Transparency

Clear pricing and an honest review system ensure trust between clients and jobbers.
